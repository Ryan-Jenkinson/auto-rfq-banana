Build an RFQ from a multi-year supplier export

Drop the incumbent supplier's multi-year PO history (xlsx export from Coupa / EAM). The app dedupes to unique items, aggregates per-window spend, and builds the candidate RFQ list for your review. Runs locally — nothing is uploaded.

Compare supplier bids

Drop each supplier's returned bid xlsx (any number of suppliers — typically 2-5). The app auto-detects the response template, matches each bid line back to the RFQ via the McMaster Part Number, and surfaces consolidation winner + carve-out exceptions + outliers.
